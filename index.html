Bill Writing Guidelines

Format Requirements

  • Bills must follow the standard NJYAG format
  • Include a clear title that describes the bill's purpose
  • Properly number all sections and subsections
  • Include definitions where necessary
  • Specify effective date and funding mechanisms

Content Guidelines

  • Address a single subject
  • Provide clear implementation details
  • Include realistic funding sources
  • Consider constitutional implications
